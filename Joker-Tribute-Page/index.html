Origins

The Birth of Chaos

The Joker's origin story has been told many times, each version more haunting than the last. What remains consistent is the tragedy that birthed Gotham's most dangerous criminal. A failed comedian, desperate to provide for his family, made one fateful decision that would change everything.

"All it takes is one bad day to reduce the sanest man alive to lunacy. That's how far the world is from where I am. Just one bad day."
- The Joker

The chemical bath that bleached his skin white, turned his hair green, and stretched his lips into a permanent grin was just the physical transformation. The real change happened in his mind - where sanity gave way to beautiful, terrifying madness.

Joker

2019 Psychological Thriller

Todd Phillips' "Joker" presents a gritty, realistic take on the iconic villain's origin story. Set in 1981 Gotham City, the film follows Arthur Fleck, a failed comedian struggling with mental illness, as he transforms into the criminal mastermind known as the Joker.
